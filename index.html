The Power of Blind Chess

Inclusion in Every Move

Chess is a universal game that sharpens the mind and fosters community. For the 43 million blind and 250 million visually impaired people worldwide, BlindMate opens the board to independent play, breaking barriers with tactile and audio innovation.

Discover Our Features

Empowering Independence

Traditional blind chess sets rely on manual pieces and sighted assistance. BlindMate's smart tactile board and AI app provide real-time feedback, allowing players to strategize and learn independently, transforming the game for accessibility.

Why BlindMate Stands Out

Tactile Projection Board

Feel every piece with our innovative board that uses tactile pins to represent moves, eliminating the need for visual input.

AI-Powered Coaching

Our mobile app offers real-time audio instructions, personalized move suggestions, and adaptive puzzles to enhance your skills.

Fully Accessible Design

Built for blind and visually impaired players, ensuring seamless gameplay without reliance on sighted assistance.

Interactive Training

Engage in customized chess puzzles and games tailored to your skill level, with audio feedback for an immersive experience.

Community Integration

Connect with chess clubs and schools for the blind, fostering social inclusion through shared gaming experiences.

Portable and Durable

Designed for portability, our board is lightweight, durable, and perfect for home or club use.
